### Runtime context lifecycle and single-owner semantics

Reviewed `packages/core/src/runtime/providerRuntimeContext.ts` and `packages/core/src/runtime/settingsRuntimeAdapter.ts`:

- `providerRuntimeContext.ts` owns only context creation/set/clear/peek/get behavior and remains settings-package agnostic.
